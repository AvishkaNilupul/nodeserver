Sell on marketplaces

Searches what other sellers charge for similar listings on each marketplace and suggests a price that undercuts the lowest credible competitor by ~5%. Tick G2G and pick its game first to include G2G.
Gameflip delivery
Each unit is one in-stock account (login + password + connect guide) attached as an auto-delivered digital code — Gameflip sends it to the buyer the moment they pay, no manual fulfilment. Gameflip listings have no stock count, so quantities above 1 are sold one at a time: when a unit sells, the site automatically relists the next one with a fresh account until the quantity is sold or you run out of stock. Accounts are reserved while a listing is live and released if you delist.
Plati / GGsell placement

GGSel placement
GGSel offers are priced in rubles — the USD price above is converted to ₽ automatically at the live rate. Pick the catalog category the offer goes in (e.g. Games → Overwatch → Twitch Drops). The offer is created and activated so it goes live; leave stock/delivery managed on GGSel as you do today.

FunPay placement
FunPay has no API — the offer is created on your account using the stored session token (set it under “Marketplace keys → FunPay”). Enter the category's node id: it's the number in the category URL, e.g. funpay.com/en/lots/2430/ for “Overwatch 2 Twitch Drops”.
Auto reserves up to Amount in-stock accounts holding this whole bundle and attaches each as one FunPay auto-delivery line (login:password), so buyers are handed one automatically; the connect guide is sent as the after-purchase message. Manual lists with the given stock — you deliver via FunPay chat.
The offer price is in your FunPay account's currency. Leave currency on USD to send the price above unchanged; pick EUR/RUB to convert it at the live rate. A price override sets the amount in that currency directly (no conversion). The offer goes live immediately unless you untick “List as active”.
