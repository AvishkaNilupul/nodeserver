Log in to the received Twitch account, then go to twitch.tv/drops/inventory and scroll to the bottom of the page, to the "Received" section.
2

3
Connect the account by following the instructions shown on the site where the connection is made.
4
Please note that after connecting, you may need to click additional buttons. For example, for Rust, you need to click the "Activate Drops" button and after that "Check for missing drops". Be careful after connecting.
